Premier National Park Tours

With our custom-made national park tours, you can visit the most beautiful places in America your way. Whether it's a backcountry adventure, a private tour with a ranger or qualified historian, or a romantic getaway, Serenity Travel offers the best national park tours available.

Yosemite

The crown jewel of the National Park system, Yosemite offers stunning vistas, soaring peaks, and more. From the world's second-highest waterfall tumbling off the highest granite monolith in the world to the picturesque beauty of Half Dome, you'll quickly see why Yosemite is one of the most-visited parks.

Mount Rainier

See the still-active volcano that dominates the Seattle skyline up close and personal. See the most heavily glaciated peak in the contiguous United States. Hike its miles of trails, see gorgeous alpine meadows, and for the truly adventurous, climb to its 14,410 foot peak.

Utah's Mighty Five

Visit Arches, Canyonlands, Bryce Canyon, Capitol Reef, and Zion in one unforgettable adventure. From world-famous rock arches to quiet canyons to one of the west's most-photographed landforms, these parks have a little of everything.

Mesa Verde

Tour the ruins of once-majestic cities built into the sides of towering cliffs. Learn about the Native Americans who lived, worked, and played here, and explore the mysteries of their civilization and what became of them.

Point Reyes NS

From elk to harbor and elephant seals, hikes to Alamere Falls and the Point Reyes Lighthouse, this National Seashore offers a truly unique experience less than an hour from San Francisco.

Lassen NVP

Lassen Volcanic National Park lets you explore the southern-most of the Cascade volcanos. Alpine meadows, hot springs, and evidence of eruptions from only a century ago highlight this, one of the least-visited but more intriguing parks.
